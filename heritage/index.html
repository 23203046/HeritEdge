Famous Temples in Tamil Nadu

Meenakshi Temple - Located in Madurai, famous for its stunning architecture and sculptures.

Brihadeeswarar Temple - A UNESCO World Heritage site built by Raja Raja Chola in Thanjavur.

Srirangam Temple - Largest temple complex in India dedicated to Lord Vishnu.

Kapaleeshwarar Temple - A vibrant Shiva temple in Mylapore, Chennai.

Ramanathaswamy Temple - Located on Rameswaram island, known for its long corridors.

Chidambaram Temple - Famous for its Nataraja shrine and unique cosmic dance significance.

Popular Tamil Nadu Foods

Dosa - A crispy pancake made from rice batter and black lentils.

Idli - Soft rice cakes served with sambar and chutney.

South Indian Thali - A variety of vegetarian dishes served on a single plate.

Chicken 65 - Spicy deep-fried chicken snack.

Sambar - A flavorful lentil-based vegetable stew.

Pongal - A popular rice dish made during harvest festival.
